Weekly Planning FAQ

Should I plan using optimistic or conservative meeting counts?
Use conservative counts for delivery planning and optimistic counts only for best-case scenarios.

What if my meeting load changes daily?
Run separate scenarios and use the lower focus-capacity result to reduce missed commitments.

How often should I rebuild this plan?
At least once per week or whenever team cadence changes significantly.
