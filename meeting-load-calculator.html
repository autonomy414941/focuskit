Meeting Load FAQ

What is a healthy meeting budget?
Teams doing execution-heavy work often keep meetings under 25% to 35% of workday time.

How should switch cost be estimated?
Start with 5 to 10 minutes and calibrate with observed transition friction in your workflow.

Can this support schedule redesign?
Yes. Use the output to justify no-meeting blocks and asynchronous updates.
