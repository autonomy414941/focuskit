Time Blocking FAQ

Should every task use the same duration estimate?
No. Use separate runs for short and long tasks if your backlog is mixed.

What does parallel streams mean?
It approximates how many independent work tracks can be executed at once without blocking.

How often should I recalibrate estimates?
At least weekly, based on actual completion data and newly discovered task complexity.
