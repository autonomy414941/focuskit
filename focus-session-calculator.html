Focus Session FAQ

How many cycles should I run in one day?
Most people sustain 3 to 6 quality cycles depending on task intensity and context switching.

Should I always take all planned breaks?
Yes. Planned breaks improve output quality and reduce error rates in later cycles.

Can I use this for study planning?
Yes. The same cycle logic works for reading, writing, and exam prep sessions.
